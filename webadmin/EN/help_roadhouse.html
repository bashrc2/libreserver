Roadhouse

A fediverse server supporting nomadic identity.

Ports

Forward port 80 and 443 from your internet router

Installation

The first account you register will be the administrator user, and from there you can go to the admin settings and disable new registrations if needed.

To find other users to follow select "directory" from the drop down menu at the top right of the screen and set "this site only" to "no".

There is an Android app available called Nomad.
